What we offer

Candle Design

Customers can request personalized candles tailored to their specifications. This could include choosing the scent, color, shape, and size of the candle.

Fragrance Blending

Customers can create their own unique scent blends for candles. This could involve selecting from a variety of fragrance oils and essential oils to create a personalized scent profile.

Gemstone Infusion

Gemstones into the candle wax, highlighting the unique feature of your product, adding a touch of mystique and emphasize the spiritual or decorative aspect of the gemstones within the candles.

Candles for Events and Occasions

Specialty candles for events and occasions such as weddings, birthdays, holidays, and corporate events. These candles may be customized with specific designs, scents, and packaging to suit the theme.

Cheryl's legacy and Our candle making Journey

At Wick and Wax Creations, we possess a unique blend of creativity, craftsmanship, and dedication to quality. Each candle is handcrafted with care and attention to detail, ensuring that every product meets our high standards of excellence. We bring together a wealth of skills and expertise in candle making, fragrance blending, and design, creating candles that are not only beautiful but also imbued with love and intention.

Founded in honor of our late sister Cheryl, our company is a testament to her passion for anything that brought light into the world. Cheryl's love for candles and the warmth they exuded inspired us to create a business that not only celebrates her memory but also spreads the same joy and comfort she brought to our lives.
